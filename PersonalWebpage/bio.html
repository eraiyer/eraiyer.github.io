Early Life and Education

I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, and came to Medford, MA to attend college at Tufts University.

Biography

I am currently a sophomore at Tufts University, where I am double majoring in computer science and economics. My interest in computer science deepened after participating in the Girls Who Code summer immersion program when I was in high school. Here, I realized the power of code and importance of empowering more girls to pursue a career in tech. Since then, I have gone on to create my own iOS app, called WorldOfMusic, TA for Girls Who Code, and become an active member of the computer science community at Tufts. Currenlty, I am a student programmer for Tufts' JumboCode, a consultant for 180 Degree Consulting, and a Teaching Assistant for Computer Science 15: Data Structures.

Hobbies

I am a member of the Tufts Club Tennis Team, a classically trained soprano, and a passionate artist.
